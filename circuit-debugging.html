Circuit Debugging

Description: Join us for the exciting Circuit Debugging event, where participants will test their knowledge and skills in electronics and circuit design. The event consists of three challenging rounds, each designed to assess different competencies related to circuit theory and practical applications.

Round 1: Basic Circuit Questions (Multiple Choice)

Participants will face a series of multiple-choice questions focused on fundamental circuit concepts. This round aims to assess your theoretical understanding of circuits.

Duration: 1 hour

Slots:

Slot 1: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Slot 2: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Round 2: Circuit Design and Analysis

In this round, participants will be tasked with designing a circuit. Questions will include determining the values of components that are suitable for attaining specified frequencies and analyzing output waveforms.

Round 3: Practical Circuit Assembly

The final round involves connecting components to build a functional circuit that meets specified output criteria. Participants will be evaluated on their practical skills and problem-solving abilities in a hands-on environment.

Objective: Demonstrate your ability to apply theoretical knowledge to practical circuit design.
