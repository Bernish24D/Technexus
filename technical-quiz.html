Technical Quiz

Event name: Technical Quiz

Total Rounds: 4

Team Size: 2 members

Round 1:

Duration: 30 minutes (11:00-11:30 AM)
Format: Pen and paper
Content: Multiple choice questions, riddles, etc.

Round 2:

Task: Guess components from displayed numbers.
Hints: 4 hints per number; fewer hints used = more points.
Elimination: Based on cumulative scores from Rounds 1 and 2.

Round 3:

Initial Points: Each team starts with a set number of points.
Format: Teams choose a product and bet points on it.
Scoring: Answer 4-5 rapid-fire questions; correct answers gain bet points, incorrect answers deduct points for the opposing team.
Elimination: Teams can be eliminated in this round.

Round 4:

Format: Buzzer round; teams answer 4-5 questions about a fixed product.
Scoring: Points for correct answers, deductions for incorrect ones.
Final Task: Identify the product from the answers provided.

Prizes: Awarded to the top three teams.
